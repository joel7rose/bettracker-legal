Effective Date: April 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Bet Tracker app, operated by BartRose Ventures LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information — email address and username when you register
  • Bet data — sports bets you log including sport, matchup, pick, odds, stake, and result
  • Group data — groups you create or join, including group name and membership

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data — how you interact with the App (screens visited, features used)
  • Device information — device type and operating system version

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Payment or financial account information
  • Precise geolocation
  • Contacts or address book
  • Social media credentials

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display your bet history, statistics, and leaderboard standings
  • Enable group features and social competition
  • Maintain and improve the App
  • Respond to support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. How We Share Your Information

Within the App

  • Your username and bet statistics (win rate, net profit, ROI) are visible to members of groups you join
  • Your email address is never visible to other users

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate the App:

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us (see Section 11). Upon deletion, your data will be removed from our systems within 30 days.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Authentication tokens stored in your device's secure keychain (never in plain storage)
  • Row-level security enforced at the database layer — users can only access their own data

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your information.

7. Children's Privacy

Bet Tracker is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18. If you believe a user under 18 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. International Users

Bet Tracker is operated by BartRose Ventures LLC from the United States. If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

9. Your Choices

  • Account deletion — you can delete your account directly in-app via Settings > Account > Delete Account. As a fallback, you may also contact us at the email in Section 11.
  • Data access — contact us to request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Opt out — you may stop using the App at any time
