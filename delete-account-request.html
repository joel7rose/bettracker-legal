Delete Your Account

Enter the email address associated with your BetCircle account. We'll send you a confirmation link to complete the deletion.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile, username, and account credentials
  • Your group memberships and competition history
  • Your notification preferences and push token
  • Your H2H challenge history

Retained (anonymized): Group chat messages and bets you logged are anonymized (your user ID is removed, not the records themselves), so other members' history and group standings are preserved. A hashed record of your account ID is kept for up to 90 days for fraud prevention, then permanently removed.
Processing time: Deletion is immediate upon confirmation.
